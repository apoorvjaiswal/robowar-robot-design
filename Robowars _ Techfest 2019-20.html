About Us


Techfest presents the International Robowars! India’s Largest Robot Combat Competition where participants from around the globe battle each other for the coveted Robowars Champion Title. Witness sparks fly as metal crushes metal in the most glorious battles interspersed with magnificent artist performances for non-stop entertainment. Brace yourselves to be a part of one of Asia’s Largest tournament here at IIT Bombay during Techfest.

Prof. Sethu Vijayakumar

Judge 2018-19

Professor Sethu Vijayakumar is a well-known Roboticist and the Director of Edinburgh Centre of Robotics. He has previously judged the world famous TV show Robot Wars since 2016. Techfest was graced to have him as the judge twice at the International Robowars at Techfest, IIT Bombay in the previous editions. He has been a recipient of numerous prestigious awards for his scientific work including the 2015 Tam Dalyell Prize for Excellence in engaging the public with science.

Mr Faruq Tauheed

Anchor 2018-19


Mr Faruq Tauheed is arguably the best combat robotics anchor and announcer in the world. He has been an announcer at the famous TV show BATTLEBOTS. We have been fortunate to have him as an anchor for the previous two editions of the International Robowars at Techfest, IIT Bombay. He has appeared in numerous TV series such as Cold Case, The Shield, NCIS and New Girl and has also lent his voice in animation projects like Mass Effect 3 and BioShock Infinite.
